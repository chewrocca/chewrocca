## 🔭 What I Do

I live and breathe **observability (o11y)** — turning the noise of distributed systems into signals that actually mean something.

I run a full **LGTM stack** to collect, ship, and make sense of telemetry at scale:

| 🟠 | Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/-Grafana-F46800?logo=grafana&logoColor=white" /> | **Grafana** | Dashboards & visualization |
| <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/-Mimir-AA00FF?logoColor=white" /> | **Mimir** | Long-term metrics storage |
| <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/-Loki-F5A623?logoColor=white" /> | **Loki** | Log aggregation |
| <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/-Tempo-E6522C?logoColor=white" /> | **Tempo** | Distributed tracing |
| <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/-Pyroscope-E91E63?logoColor=white" /> | **Pyroscope** | Continuous profiling |

Instrumented with **OpenTelemetry** end-to-end — because vendor lock-in is so last decade.

> *Making complex problems easier to understand, one dashboard at a time.*

## 🤖 AI & The Future

I'm fascinated by how **AI is reshaping observability** — from AIOps and anomaly detection to LLM-powered root cause analysis. I experiment with AI tooling daily, exploring how large language models can cut through alert fatigue, summarize incidents, and help engineers focus on what matters.
